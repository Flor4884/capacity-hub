Who We Are

Global Capacity Management ensures the effective alignment of talent supply with business demand across all shores. The function provides end-to-end visibility into workforce availability, upcoming releases, and staffing needs, enabling proactive and data-driven decision-making.

Through standardized processes and centralized tracking — including bench management, release planning, and redeployment strategies — we minimize idle time, optimize resource utilization, and reduce operational risk.

Global Capacity enables greater predictability, improved margins, and scalable growth across the organization.

Our Mission

Enable business growth by strategically aligning workforce capacity with demand, optimizing utilization, and ensuring execution excellence.

Processes & Workflows

How We Operate

Our core processes ensure every consultant in bench has a structured path — and every decision is documented, reviewed, and aligned with the business.

Bench Workflow

End-to-end process from release to redeployment

1
Delivery

Release Form

Delivery submits a Release Form minimum 30 days before a consultant's end date, triggering the bench review workflow.

2
Capacity

Capacity Review

Capacity evaluates bench status, skill profile, and redeployment potential. Decision: Keep on bench or proceed to termination.

3
Practice Lead

Practice Approval

For terminations, the Practice Leader reviews and approves before any action is taken. No termination proceeds without sign-off.

4
Capacity + Practice

Training Plan

For bench consultants, a personalized Training Plan is assigned with the Technical PoC — covering upskilling, reskilling, or gap reduction.

5
Capacity

Redeployment

Capacity actively works with portfolios and delivery to find the next project. Regular check-ins track progress until the consultant is placed.
